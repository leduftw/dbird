These five sound-effect files are byte-identical to the files in the signed
Flappy Bird 1.3 Android release by .GEARS Studios:

- `sfx_die.ogg`
- `sfx_hit.ogg`
- `sfx_point.ogg`
- `sfx_swooshing.ogg`
- `sfx_wing.ogg`

They are included only to reproduce the original game's audio. They are not
covered by any license that may apply to dbird's source code. All rights in
these sound files remain with their respective owner.

The source APK has SHA-256
`a3e6958ce2100966f4e207778e4cdbe72788214148c7f4bfd042ba365498deb3`.
The extracted files were cross-checked against the preserved files at
<https://github.com/THEN00P/reFlappy/tree/bdda48a6b5166aabf7b4ec578a27bc88525de26d/app/src/main/assets/sounds>.
